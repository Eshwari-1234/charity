Cloth Box

Clothing donation bins are a convenient and easy way for people to donate their gently used clothes. They are typically operated by charitable organizations who use the donations to provide funding for various causes. When the clothes donated by people arrive at the Clothes Box Foundation, they go through a system of processing. They are first divided into clothes that are fit to be distributed and clothes that can't be. Segregating clothes by size is crucial to the process. It helps the foundation send the right clothes to the right places.
