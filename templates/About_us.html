Know More



Our old age home is a residential institution where elderly people who require assistance with daily activities and healthcare needs can live. We provide various services such as meals, medical care, social activities, and assistance with personal hygiene. Our aim is to ensure the well-being and comfort of our residents who may not have adequate support at home. We also have an orphanage which is a residential institution that provides care, housing, and support for children who have lost their parents or have been abandoned. We offer a safe and nurturing environment where children receive food, shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support. The staff typically includes caregivers, social workers, and educators who work to meet the physical, emotional, and developmental needs of the children. Our aim is to provide stability and opportunities for children to thrive until they can be placed in permanent homes through adoption, fostering, or reunification with relatives.
